About Us

Located in the heart of Austin, our food truck serves up Southern- and Cajun-inspired dishes with a modern twist. Try our crispy chicken sandwiches — hot dipped, if you dare! Wash it all down with a cold beverage from our curated drink list, featuring local brews, handcrafted cocktails, and a signature slushie lineup.

Don’t forget to ask about our loyalty program! We also cater and do events!
